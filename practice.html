About Matt Hickman

I am currentlty a student at the University of San Diego. I started taking pictures last summer and have been in love with it ever since. Spending lots of time in both Portland and San Diego gives me a unique perspective and countless opportunities for interesting pictures. Some of my favorite things to shoot are cityscapes, interesting portraits, and lights.

I have plans to improve my photography skills by finding new and creative ways to express myself. Much more to come . . . stay tuned
