About Matt Hickman

Matt Hickman is currentlty a student at the University of San Diego. He started taking pictures last summer and has been in love with it ever since. Spending lots of time in both Portland and San Diego gives Matt unique perspective and opportunities for new pictures. Some of Matt's favorite things to shoot are cityscapes, interesting portraits, and lights. Matt has plans to improve his photography skills by finding new and creative ways to express himself. Much more to come... stay tuned.
